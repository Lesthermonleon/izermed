Data Privacy Policy

Introduction

IzerMed Pharmacy is committed to providing you with quality pharmaceutical services, including prescription and over-the-counter medications, health products, and personal care. Our mission is to make your shopping experience as convenient and secure as possible, both online and in-store. We respect your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA) and other relevant regulations.

IzerMed Pharmacy (through its website, izermed.com.ph) is committed to the protection and management of your personal data that you voluntarily provide. This Privacy Policy will explain how we process your personal data and what rights you have regarding it.

Why Do We Collect Your Personal Data?

We collect personal data to ensure that we provide you with an enhanced online shopping experience. The information you share with us allows us to fulfill your orders, improve our services, and notify you about promotions, discounts, and other services.

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate online orders and deliveries to your home or office.
  • To create and manage your IzerMed Pharmacy account, allowing you to:
    • Store items in your shopping cart between visits.
    • Proceed through checkout faster.
    • Check order status and history.
    • Update your account and shipping details.
    • Submit product reviews and feedback.
    • Receive promotional offers and newsletters.
  • To improve our customer experience through surveys and analysis.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

What Type of Personal Data We Collect

Depending on the services you use, we may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Basic personal information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing addresses, and account credentials (username and password).
  • Sensitive personal information: Date of birth, gender, occupation, and medical history.
  • Payment details: Credit card information and billing address.
  • Transaction data: Order history, products purchased, and feedback.

Please ensure that the personal data you provide is accurate, complete, and up-to-date.

Cookies and Web Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device to remember preferences, improve website functionality, and track usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may affect the functionality of our website.

We also use third-party analytics tools to analyze website traffic. These tools collect non-identifiable information such as:

  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Time and date of visit
  • Referring websites
  • Web browser type and operating system
This data helps us improve our services and is not shared with other parties.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted partners, service providers, or contractors who assist with fulfilling orders, processing payments, or delivering services.
  • With law enforcement or government agencies, as required by law.
  • As necessary to comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

How We Protect Your Personal Data

We implement strong security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Secure servers with encryption.
  • Restricting access to authorized personnel.
  • Safeguarding payment information by only displaying the last four digits of your credit card number.
  • Keeping your personal data in secure locations with limited access.

How Long We Keep Your Personal Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. When no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

From time to time, IzerMed Pharmacy may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in laws or our services. Any revisions will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will take effect immediately. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Access, Correction, and Deletion of Your Personal Data

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our website or via email at privacy@izermed.com.ph.
